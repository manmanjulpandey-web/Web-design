About Us

Where Inner Peace
Meets Outer Vitality

IndoGulf Yoga Wellness, in association with Prachi Fitness and Yoga Instructions (CR 109026-01), is committed to delivering quality training programs through the collaboration and resources of eminent teachers across various areas of wellness and fitness.

Born from a vision to bring authentic Traditional Yoga from India to the Gulf, IndoGulf Yoga Wellness has steadily built a distinguished presence in Bahrain since 2005. With an ongoing program operating in leading hospitality chains and fitness centers — including the Gulf Hotel and Crowne Plaza — the organization has introduced hundreds of people to the transformative power of Traditional Hatha Yoga.

"Our organization provides the finest masters and trainers in the Kingdom of Bahrain — dedicated to delivering the best in their area of specialization, through an affiliate network of global yoga and fitness programs."

IndoGulf Yoga Wellness is a humble endeavor towards meeting the needs of health clubs and facilities that possess the required infrastructure for group or personal training but lack the specialized manpower. The organization bridges this gap by supplying elite, certified trainers and pioneering wellness events across the Kingdom.

Yoga Programs

The Art of
Traditional Yoga

Rooted in the ancient wisdom of India, IndoGulf's yoga curriculum spans classical and contemporary styles — each designed to harmonize body, breath, and mind.

Hatha Yoga

A perfect blend of gentle postures and mindful breathing to harmonize body and mind. The cornerstone of IndoGulf's traditional offering.

Vinyasa Yoga

Balance your body and center your mind through flowing sequences that connect movement to breath for a healthier, happier you.

Kundalini Yoga

Awakening the senses where every breath guides you inward — a powerful practice for spiritual energy and inner transformation.

Intuitive Yoga

Let your body guide you, your breath center you, and your spirit rise in this free-flowing, body-led practice.

Aroma Yoga

Gentle movements, aromatic calm, and the awakening of your spirit through the integration of therapeutic essential oils.

Slow Yoga

Flow slowly, breathe deeply, and let your energy rise like morning light in this restorative, mindful practice.

Beyond asana, IndoGulf also offers Yoga Nidra, Meditation, Stress Management Programs, and Mentorship initiatives — forming a holistic pathway to mental clarity and emotional resilience.

Swimming Academy

Learn to Swim in
10 Hours

IndoGulf Wellness Swimming Academy provides expert swimming instruction for all ages and genders — fostering confidence, water safety, and a lifelong love of swimming in a safe, supportive environment.

The Academy is staffed by certified male and female professional coaches, making it one of Bahrain's few facilities offering dedicated ladies-only batches with exclusively female instructors. Programs are tailored to diverse needs — from first-time swimmers to competitive athletes.

Group ClassesFun, social, and engaging — learn alongside others in structured group sessions.
Private LessonsPersonalized, one-on-one instruction tailored to individual skill level and goals.
Ladies-Only BatchesExclusive sessions with certified female coaches in a comfortable, private setting.
Competitive TrainingAdvanced performance programs for swimmers looking to compete at higher levels.
Cabin Crew SwimmingEssential water safety and confidence training for aviation professionals.
Family BatchesJoint sessions with special family discount packages for all-age participation.
Expat Swimming CoursesA welcoming program for newcomers to Bahrain — meet fellow expats while learning.
Holiday CoursesIntensive holiday programs for children and adults during school break periods.

Specialist services also include Hydrotherapy, Aqua Aerobics, and Scuba Diving / Sea Beach Corporate Training with certified instructors and official certification upon completion.
